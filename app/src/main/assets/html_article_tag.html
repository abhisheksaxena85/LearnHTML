The HTML <article> tag defines an independent self-contained content in a document, page, application or a site.

The article tag content makes sense on its own. It is independent and complete from each other content shown on the page. This tag is generally used on Forum post, Blog post, News story, comment etc.

HTML article tag example

Example:

Code Example

<article>
<h4>Narendra Modi</h4> <i>Prime Minister Of Bharat</i> <p>Narendra DamodarDas Modi is the 15th and current Prime Minister of Bharat.</p>
</article>

Output:

Narendra Modi

Prime Minister Of Bharat

Narendra DamodarDas Modi is the 15th and current Prime Minister of Bharat.

HTML article tag also supports global and event attributes in HTML.
